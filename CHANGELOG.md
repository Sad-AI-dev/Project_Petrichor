# Changelog
This is the changelog for **Project Petrichor**.  
All notable additions, removals and chagnes will be documented on this page.

## [v.0.2.0] - Creature Update - 17-05-2026
This is the first major update for **Project Petrichor**.  
The update mainly adds the creature pages and fixes some minor issues.  

### Added
New Pages:

- **104** Creature Pages
	- **63** Monster Pages
	- **37** Boss Monster Pages
	- **4** Other Creature Pages

- **21** Family Pages
	- These contain minor insights into the various species on **Petrichor V**

- Missing item lore notes:
	- Encrypted Cerebellum
	- Exposed Cerebellum
	- Prison Matrix
	- Sentry Key

### Changed

- Creatures Canvas
	- As mentioned in the [v.0.1.0] update, this canvas had a temporary layout.
	- This layout has been changed to sort creatures into individual species and "families".

### Removed

- [Item Page] Super Massive Leach
	- Removed line referrencing the `Massive Leach` item, as it no longer exists due to being renamed in *Risk of Rain Returns*.

### Fixed

- [Elite Aspect Page] Of one Mind
	- Fixed line describing the origin of the power having some temp text.

- Various typos and broken references
