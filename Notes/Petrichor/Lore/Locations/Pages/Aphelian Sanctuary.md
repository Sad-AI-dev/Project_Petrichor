# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This is the translation of an inscription found on [[Petrichor V]]. The translation was generated by the [[FIELDTECH Image-To-Text Translator (v2.5.10b)]] program.

The inscription was created by an older member of the [[Dunepeople of Aphelia]], who were saved from the [[Tar]] by [[Providence]] and brought to [[Petrichor V]].

The writer comments that the younger members have forgotten the [[Tar]]. How they question their need to cleanse themselves.

The wording used helps establish a more personal retelling of the events that took place on [[Aphelia]]. 
The established current need for cleansing shows that the [[Tar]] remains a treat to these people, although it may be closer to a spiritual ritual, as the youth doesn't understand it's purpose.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
As the name suggests, this location was likely created by [[Providence]] to serve as a sanctuary for the [[Dunepeople of Aphelia]], where they would be shielded from the [[Tar]].

During gameplay, no [[Dunepeople of Aphelia]] can be encountered. The presence of members of the [[Clay Family]] points to a grim theory that the [[Dunepeople of Aphelia]] fell victim to the [[Tar]] once more on [[Petrichor V]], possibly after [[Providence]] passed away.
