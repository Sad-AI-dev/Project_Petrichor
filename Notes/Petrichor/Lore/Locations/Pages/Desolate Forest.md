# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore">Crashing into a verdant field isn't the worst outcome. I can see lush, rolling hills and strange castles built into mountains. There are foreign species patrolling the grasslands and bizarre housing foundations erected along worn footpaths. I've seen lizards who walk like men and otherworldly stone creatures infused by blazing lights. They seem docile and intelligent, passing by one another with little interaction, but their stiffened postures leave me wary. There's so much culture here for an undocumented planet. There's so much history to these buildings, but their construction doesn't make any sense. Rebar and concrete make up juvenile fabrications, accompanied by worn bricks gathered from ancient castles just meters away. Tree stumps and dead branches litter the landscape as if a forest was felled. But decay settled on this garden long ago... How do you get to concrete before raising a single village? Red sparks dance in the distant sky. I don't know what I'm hoping for.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
After the [[UES Contact Light]] was attacked and taken down by [[Providence]], [[The Survivor]] took a [[Drop Pod]] and crash landed in the [[Desolate Forest]].

They note various structures, formations and creatures in their surroundings. [[The Survivor]] notes lush hills littered with tree stumps and dead branches. As if a forest was felled long ago.

[[The Survivor]] spots multiple [[Colossus|Colossi]] nestled into the distant mountains, although they are unaware of their true form, describing them as "strange castles".

Various inhabitants of [[Petrichor V]] are spotted by [[The Survivor]]. [[Lemurian|Lemurians]] and [[Stone Golem|Stone Golems]] are specifically mentioned. [[The Survivor]] notes that they seem intelligent and docile, and that they pass each other with little interaction.

Before the incident of the [[UES Contact Light]], [[Petrichor V]] was an undocumented planet.

[[The Survivor]] notes that the structures they find don't make sense. Some are build with concrete and rebar, while others are constructed of ancient worn bricks.

Finally, they mention seeing red sparks dance in the distant sky. This is in reference to the particles emitted in the surroundings of a [[Teleporter]].

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
[[Desolate Forest]] is one of the possible starting locations during gameplay.
