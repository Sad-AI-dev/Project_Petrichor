# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] reached the [[Damp Caverns]] after using a [[Teleporter]].
The only light within the [[Damp Caverns]] are the bioluminescent fungi that grow here.

[[The Survivor]] encountered multiple [[Mushrum|Mushrums]], suggesting that this is their natural environment.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
[[Damp Caverns]] lays below the surface of [[Petrichor V]]. 
A great number of sinkholes lead down into these caverns. The ground and walls are mostly covered in fungal growths, which give off a terrible smell.
