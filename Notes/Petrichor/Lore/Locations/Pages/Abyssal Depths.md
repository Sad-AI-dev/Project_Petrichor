# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a transcription of an audio fragment generated by the [[DataScraper (v3.1.53 - beta branch)]] program.

The log appears to be a messily recorded audio message from a survivor that landed in the [[Abyssal Depths]] just a few minutes ago. They were likely a survivor from the [[UES Contact Light]], as they do not recognize the members of the [[Lemurian Family]] or [[Magma Worm|Magma Worms]] they encounter.

The formatting makes the information hard to interpret, but breaking the message up into smaller segments helps. The fragments get shorter and less detailed as the message goes on, showing the rapidly deteriorating situation surrounding the survivor.

`We have landed a few minutes ago and I have no time.`
This shows when the message was recorded. It also shows that the survivor feared they could die at any moment and thus had no time to properly write a log entry.

`I must log as quickly as possible, I hope the speech-to-text works.`
This repeats the reason for the formatting of the message and confirms that these were hastily spoken lines before the survivor likely died.

`Many died on impact, inside hollow cavern.`
Many survivors that landed in the [[Abyssal Depths]] immediately died on impact. This suggests that not all drop pods were able to safely land their occupants on the surface of [[Petrichor V]].
The survivor goes on to describe their surroundings, starting with the fact that they are underground.

`Chains, great chains, fire`
More vague descriptions of the [[Abyssal Depths]].

`The ground, they come from the ground, the burning, they breathe fire.`
This is likely in reference to the members of the [[Lemurian Family]] and how they appear from their tunnel network.

`We are hiding beneath the caverns.`
The survivor is not alone, and they are hiding in a lower section of the area.

`Groans, they coax metal from the surface with song.`
They hear noises above them, likely from the members of the [[Lemurian Family]]. The members of the [[Lemurian Family]] are known for their rich culture including song and dance. It appears that they may be able to summon [[Brass Contraption|Brass Contraptions]] using song. 
This would explain the role that the [[Brass Contraption]] plays on [[Petrichor V]] and how it's existence is interlinked with the [[Lemurian Family]].

`Giant snakes made of fire.`
The survivor is now listing off various threats near them. This line refers to a [[Magma Worm]].

`They collect the security chests.`
This is possibly in reference to the [[Scavenger]], who collects the items from the security chests. It is possible that they are still just collecting the chests themselves, as the crash happened mere moments ago.

`The death god.`
This is most likely a reference to [[N'kuhana]]. It is unlikely that the survivor saw the actual representation of [[N'kuhana]] themselves, and rather observed their presence in some indirect way.

`We are in hell, here chains keeps the ground from splintering.`
This shows the survivor's state of mind. The notion that the ground is splintering, and the giant chains are the only thing preventing this is curious, but should be taken with a grain of salt considering the state the survivor is in at the time of recording.

`They breathe beneath and above and around us.`
This is likely a reference to the breath attack from the [[Elder Lemurian]] who is attacking them.

`Punishments it must be, forgive me.`
The survivor believes their situation to be some kind of divine punishment. They ask for forgiveness.

`I cannot see, too dark.`
Either their lights just went out, or it is too dark to see in their hiding spot.

`Deep thunder.`
This *could* be a reference to the [[Overloading Worm]], but it is unclear as only a sound of thunder was described.

`Goodbye.`
The survivors final word.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
The [[Abyssal Depths]] are likely a set of caverns located deep underneath the surface of [[Petrichor V]].
