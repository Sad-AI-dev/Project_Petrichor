# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] travelled through the [[Ancient Valley]] at some point in their journey. They mention that visibility is particularly bad in this region. The fog and blizzard make it hard to see further than 2 meters in front of them, their mounted lights seem to hardly help with this.

[[The Survivor]] mentions hearing a stampede in the distance behind them. This noise is possibly caused by a herd of [[Bighorn Bison|Bighorn Bisons]]. [[The Survivor]] also notices scorched steel along the bridge, which was likely created by [[Ifrit]].

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
The [[Ancient Valley]] is located in a mountainous tundra region of [[Petrichor V]]. Large bridges connect pathways between the mountains.
