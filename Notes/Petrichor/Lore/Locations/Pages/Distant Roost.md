# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log was generated by the [[DataScraper (v3.1.53 - beta branch)]] program.

The log is a voice recording created by the [[Captain]] of the [[UES Safe Travels]].
It details the events that took place in the first 24 hours after the [[UES Safe Travels]] started its mission on [[Petrichor V]].

Crewmembers were deployed either in larger groups via [[Shuttle|Shuttles]] or in smaller squads through [[Drop Pod|Drop Pods]].
[[Captain]] mentions that he has considered joining them on [[Petrichor V]] to oversee the operation, but has chosen to remain on the bridge of the [[UES Safe Travels]] for now.

The [[Shuttle|Shuttles]] landed in the [[Distant Roost]]. [[Shuttle|Shuttles]] can be found in many other [[Locations.canvas|Locations]] during gameplay, suggesting that either not all [[Shuttle|Shuttles]] landed here, or they later moved to different regions.

The crewmembers aboard the [[Shuttle|Shuttles]] attempted to establish a rally point, but were attacked by local fauna, suffering heavy casualties.

[[Captain]] comments that he is thankful that UES granted his request for ammunition and armaments. He also mentions that the crew have reported "strange and irregular natural phenomenon."

The log ends with [[Captain]] gazing at [[Commencement]] from the [[UES Safe Travels]], commenting that this will make for a good story.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
[[Distant Roost]] is located in a mountainous region near one of the oceans of [[Petrichor V]].
