# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a monologue from [[Mithrix]] directed towards his brother [[Providence]].

The first line: `Have you fogotten - that those gates are my design?` refers to "gates".
This is likely in reference to the same gates mentioned in the [[A Moment, Fractured]] log.
The "gates" could be the structure seen in [[A Moment, Fractured]], however, it is notable that the log refers to them in plural.
It is possible to interpret the "gates" to be in reference to the [[Teleporter|Teleporters]] seen throughout the franchise, as [[Providence]] uses one to cross space during the intro cutscene of *Risk of Rain Returns*.

The next paragraph shows that [[Providence]] only paid attention to the inhabitants of [[Petrichor V]] and didn't seem to care for what was taking place on [[Commencement]].

[[Mithrix]] claims to have been working on a grander design for the "gates". After all, they were his designs to begin with. The [[A Moment, Fractured]] log mentions that the "gates" could only have been realized by [[Providence]], which may explain why it has taken [[Mithrix]] so long to create a new one.
[[Mithrix]] claims that the new "gate" is capable to traveling even greater distances. This is possibly the purpose of the [[Teleporter]] seen on [[Commencement]]. This also gives insight into what [[Mithrix|Mithrix's]] plan was.

[[Mithrix]] goes on to taunt [[Providence]], explaining how he would take his revenge. He would undermine [[Providence|Providence's]] wish to save the creatures of dying planets. [[Mithrix]] would destroy them, become the reason for their planet's death, before [[Providence]] would get a chance to save them.

Finally, we get some insight into [[Providence]] and how he has been paying special attention to a specific planet of water and dirt recently. [[Mithrix]] suggests that he may destroy that planet first.

The planet [[Providence]] is studying is somewhat vague, but may be in reference to [[Earth]] and the human race. This would suggest that [[Earth]] has reached a critical state, and [[Providence]] believes that humanity may cause their own downfall through climate change.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
The name "[[Commencement]]" is notable. It suggests that the location represents a beginning, the start of a story. A possible theory is that this location represents the beginning of [[Mithrix]] in the form encountered during gameplay. Before being banished to [[Commencement]], [[Mithrix]] never harboured any ill will towards [[Providence]]. It is only after being banished by him that he starts to morph into an antagonist.

[[Commencement]] is located on the moon of [[Petrichor V]], and is densely detailed with points of interest.

Large statues line the walls of the hallway leading outside. It is unclear what these statues represent.

4 types of pillars can be found across the stage, each representing one of the 4 [[Fundamental Elements]]: [[Fundamental Element: Mass|Mass]], [[Fundamental Element: Design|Design]], [[Fundamental Element: Blood|Blood]], and [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]].

A [[Shuttle]] (which is referred to as the "Rescue Ship") landed on [[Commencement]]. 
A large number of [[Commando]] corpses litter the floor around the [[Shuttle]], but it is still operational.
Unfortunately, it appears that [[Mithrix]] has placed a protective barrier around it, preventing survivors from using it to return to the [[UES Safe Travels]], which can be spotted in the sky.
