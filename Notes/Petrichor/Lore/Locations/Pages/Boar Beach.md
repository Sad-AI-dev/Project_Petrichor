# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] discovered the [[Boar Beach]] while exploring the [[Ancient Valley]].

There seems to be a large number of [[Boarlit|Boarlits]] present. A structure similar to a fence marks the border of their territory.
