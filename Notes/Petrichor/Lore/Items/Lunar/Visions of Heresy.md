# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This is a fragment from the [[The Evisceration of Kur-skan the Heretic]] texts.

The text speaks of the [[Heretic]], who was punished for her betrayal, lies and scheming ways.
As punishment, she was torn to pieces and scattered across the farthest depths of the Moon.

First, her eyes were taken and sealed in boiling glass, forced to gaze upon her failure.
