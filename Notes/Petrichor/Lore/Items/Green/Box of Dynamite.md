# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This item is a box of explosives being shipped to `"The Government"`.

The individual sending this is a `Gold-level VIP`, suggesting that UES offers various tiers of privilege to their customers.

The process for shipping an item is completely automated, allowing subtle shipments such as a *box of explosives* to be shipped without issue.

This item also summons the [[Lt. Droneboy]] drone.

As a fun reference, the delivery date is the same as the 1.0 release date for *Risk of Rain 2*.
