# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a brief snippet of the [[Brief History of Interplanetary Advances]], Vol.2 book.

This shows that the Fuel Cell served an essential purpose in the technological advancements required to realize interplanetary travel.

It is also shown that the Fuel Cell is a commonplace energy source and is rather unstable in nature. Since the development of the Fuel Cell, better alternatives have been developed.
