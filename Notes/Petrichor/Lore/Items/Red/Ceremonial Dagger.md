# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is continued in the [[Wetland Aspect]] log. This shows how the log likely takes place near the [[Wetland Aspect]].

The log shows how the Ceremonial Dagger is magical in nature and requires sacrifice to be activated.

Additionally, `Marion` says: `She will save us`. The "She" mentioned refers to [[N'kuhana]].
