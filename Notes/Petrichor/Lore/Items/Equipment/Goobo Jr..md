# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a transcription of a conversation between [[Richard D. Baggins]] and a survivor called `Mike` which was recorded aboard the [[UES [REDACTED]|UES [REDACTED].]]

This fragment has been flagged for review by the military court in a case against [[Richard D. Baggins]].

The log shows that [[Goobo Jr.]] was created as a science experiment. It's main consistency is described as `slime` and it leaves stains.
Uniforms supplied by UES have a quirk resulting in stains being hard to remove from them.

[[Goobo Jr.]] was likely created by a survivor named `Mike`. [[Goobo Jr.]] is incapable of speech, but can mimic any creature at will. In order for [[Goobo Jr.]] to take on the appearance of another creature, it must observe if for a short period of time.

It is unclear what [[Goobo Jr.]] was instructed to do while taking on the appearance of [[Richard D. Baggins]], but it is possible that this is what caused him to be the subject of a court martial.
