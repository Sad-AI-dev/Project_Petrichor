# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Plasma Shrimp]] is the void-corrupted version of the [[AtG Missile Mk. 1]].

This log is a fragment from a [[Lost Journal]], which was recovered from [[Petrichor V]].

The survivor praises attacking from behind great defenses.
The underlying message here seems to be to prioritize survival, regardless of methodology used.
