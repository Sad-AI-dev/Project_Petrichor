# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log contains a set of internal logs from a member of the [[Solus Family]] detailing the design details of the [[Prison Matrix]].

The purpose of the [[Prison Matrix]] is stated to be used for "Divergent Unit Containment". It is unclear if the "Divergent Units" are referencing members of the [[Solus Family]] that have resisted becoming part of [[The Collective]] or if it simply references all entities outside [[The Collective]].

The [[Prison Matrix]] is revealed to be a power source, which uses nuclear fission to generate power.
The warning `(!DO NOT RELEASE CONTENTS INTO COLLECTIVE NETWORK!)` gives credit to the idea that the [[Prison Matrix]] is, in itself, a prison designed to contain members of the [[Solus Family]] that oppose or resist [[The Collective]].

The final line shows that this [[Prison Matrix]] was not part of the original programming of the [[Solus Family]], but was rather designed by [[The Collective]] after being corrupted by [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]].
