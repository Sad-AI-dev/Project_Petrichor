# <span class="lore-title">Notes</span>
A cut of meat from a [[Bighorn Bison]].
The meat is being shipped to a catering service on earth. The bison has been treated with special antibiotics.

This shows that the [[Bighorn Bison]] has been domesticated by humans, but is a rare livestock, requiring interspatial shipping. The mention of antibiotics ensuring exceptional growth shows advanced bio engineering technology.
