# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log is a comment from [[Mithrix]] on the design of the [[Lunar Chimera (Exploder)]], as they were created by him.

The [[Lunar Chimera (Exploder)|Exploder]] is extremely simple in design and incredibly efficient in material usage. However, the quality of the components used makes them unstable. [[Mithrix]] decided to take this flaw and turn in into purpose, making the falling apart of the chimera an integral part in how it functions.

The [[Lunar Chimera (Exploder)]] portrays elements of various other creatures seen on [[Petrichor V]]. Visually, it most resembles the [[Mechanical Spider|Mechanical Spiders]] that were brought in by the [[UES Contact Light]]. It even has a similar, albeit more elaborate version, attack to that of a [[Mechanical Spider]] in the projectiles it shoots.
It explodes upon coming close to a survivor, similar to the way a [[Jellyfish]] would, and leaves a lingering harmful effect, similar to the effect left by the [[Mushrum]].
