*Macrobic Predator* is a monster from *Risk of Rain Returns*, belonging to the [[Macrobe Family]].
For all monsters, see the [[Creatures.canvas|Creatures]] page.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore"><span class="lore-text-dark">Field Notes:</span>
Some kind of massive insect, this creature snaps like a viper and skitters like an ant. It shouldn't be possible at such a scale to move so abruptly and yet it does. Similar to the Macrobe, this monster is some kind of predatory cell. With a massive rope of nerves at its core, I can only image it acts on instinct alone. I dug into a corpse to see if the spinal rope could sustain me in this toxic hive, but I found my arms stuck to the thick membrane and I had to use my knife to remove the non-newtonian gel. It'd make a great trap for prey, but I don't have time to test that theory.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] encountered the [[Macrobic Predator]] in the [[Hive Cluster]]. This is notable because they don't appear here during gameplay.

The [[Macrobic Predator]] moves far quicker than would be expected from its size. [[The Survivor]] was running out of food in the [[Hive Cluster]], so they attempted to harvest the nerve bundle and the core of the [[Macrobic Predator]], but got their arm stuck in the membrane.

The membrane acts like a non-Newtonian gel. [[The Survivor]] theorizes that this element could make for a great trap for other prey, but they didn't have the time to test this theory.
