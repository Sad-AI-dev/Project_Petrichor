*Evolved Lemurian* is a monster from *Risk of Rain Returns*, belonging to the [[Lemurian Family]].
For all monsters, see the [[Creatures.canvas|Creatures]] page.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore"><span class="lore-text-dark">Field Notes:</span>
Could this be a distant cousin of the Lemurians below? An ancient evolutionary leap? These airborne reptiles do not interact with the ground Lemurians except to harass them occasionally. Perhaps there is some kind of natural feud between the species?

It is the membranous skin that attaches their long arms to their body which enables their flight. Each wingtip culminates in vicious talons. When they come in flocks these winged Lemurians are capable of great carnage.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
It is unclear when [[The Survivor]] encountered the [[Evolved Lemurian|Evolved Lemurians]], but they did note the unique relationship between the [[Evolved Lemurian|Evolved Lemurians]] and the [[Lemurian|Lemurians]].

While the [[Evolved Lemurian|Evolved Lemurians]] can fly, the [[Lemurian|Lemurians]] can not. It seems that this rift in physicality happened some time ago, as the [[Evolved Lemurian|Evolved Lemurians]] seem to be in some kind of conflict with the [[Lemurian|Lemurians]]. The [[Evolved Lemurian|Evolved Lemurians]] can occasionally be seen harassing the [[Lemurian|Lemurians]].
