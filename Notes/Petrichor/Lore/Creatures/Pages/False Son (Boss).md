# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log is a monologue from [[Mithrix]] commenting on the creation of the [[False Son]], questioning [[Providence]] for allowing such a thing to happen.

It is clear that [[Mithrix]] is not aware of [[Providence|Providence's]] passing at the time that [[False Son]] was created.

[[Mithrix]] calls [[False Son]] a `mockery of a greater being`. This shows that [[Mithrix]] refers to his own species as `greater beings`.
The tools used to create [[False Son]] are mentioned to be beyond the understanding of the denizens of [[Petrichor V]].

The [[False Son]] is found at the top of [[Prime Meridian]], at the peak of the [[Path of the Colossus]].
