*Lunar Chimera (Wisp)* is a monster from *Risk of Rain 2*, belonging to the [[Lunar Family]].
For all monsters, see the [[Creatures.canvas|Creatures]] page.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore">Soul driven. Of course. As with all vermin - let us begin with fire.

Free axes of movement – wonderful design. I will highlight that.

Slow acceleration – that can be improved. Speed is war.

Feeble combat skills. Treatable. Those Templars may be of inspiration.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log is a comment from [[Mithrix]] on the design of the [[Lunar Chimera (Wisp)]], as they were created by him.

Unlike the [[Lunar Chimera (Golem)]], [[Mithrix]] points out that the design of the [[Lunar Chimera (Wisp)]] is driven by [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]], though the notion that speed is a must in war remains.

The [[Lunar Chimera (Wisp)]] is largely inspired by the design of the [[Greater Wisp]], but their attacks are inspired by the [[Clay Templar|Clay Templars]].
