# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] spotted a [[Colossus]] before they used their first [[Teleporter]].
This shows that [[The Survivor]] used their drop pod as a base of operations for a short while before eventually venturing out further using a [[Teleporter]].

The [[Colossus]] is similar to the [[Stone Golem]], but has a size similar to that of a mountain. It takes a [[Colossus]] a few days to awaken, slowly morphing the landscape as they rise from the ground.

[[The Survivor]] calls the sight of a [[Colossus]] rising from the ground "simply ridiculous".
