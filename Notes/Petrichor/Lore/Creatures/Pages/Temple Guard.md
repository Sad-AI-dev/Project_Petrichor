# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore"><span class="lore-text-dark">Field Notes:</span>

I cannot believe this is indeed a machine- and yet it is made from some alloy and uses heavy artillery! Was this on the ship, or are these guards a naturally occuring 'species' here? # <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] most likely encountered the [[Temple Guard|Temple Guards]] in the [[Temple of the Elders]].

The [[Temple Guard]] is capable of assembling itself and is a machine made of some unspecified alloy. It attacks using heavy artillery attacks and seems to have been created for the purpose of preventing hostiles from entering a certain area.

[[The Survivor]] questions if these were brought in by the [[UES Contact Light]], but the lack of a manufacturer's signature suggests that these were created by [[Providence]].

This note also confirms that [[The Survivor]] has a scanner in their suit capable of determining which manufacturer created the object. This, in turn, shows that most items created by major manufacturer's add some kind of signature to the item.
It is possible that the scanner also provides additional details about the scanned item.
