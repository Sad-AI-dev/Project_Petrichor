# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a monologue from a [[Scavenger]], which was translated by the [[MyBabel Machine Translator]] algorithm.

The [[Scavenger]] watches a group of survivors. They want their items, but recognize the danger in fighting them. The [[Scavenger]] knows that the survivors are known to kill without mercy.

The [[Scavenger]] mentions that their belongings bring them comfort. Their favorite item is likely a [[Tougher Times]], or possible even a [[Tough Times]] that got worn down over time. 
This means that `Malik` from the [[Tougher Times]] log will need to defeat this [[Scavenger]] in order to find what they are looking for.

The [[Scavenger]] goes on to say that the "others" informed them that the survivors came from the sky, and that they brought many things with them. They go on to speculate that they must have a home they came from, where a great deal of many things must be. The [[Scavenger]] makes it their ultimate goal to find all those things. In order to achieve this, it must fight.

This shows that unlike most creatures on [[Petrichor V]], the [[Scavenger]] is not inherently aligned with [[Providence]]. 
They do not fight to protect the planet. Instead, they fight so they can find more things. If communication were possible, they could even make very shifty allies to the survivors.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Scavenger]] was likely on of the last creatures [[The Survivor]] encountered on [[Petrichor V]].
Their habit of scavenging the various [[Items.canvas|Items]] on the planet makes them in some ways very similar to [[The Survivor]]. 

They lament having to kill each other, as they had hoped to find an ally.
[[The Survivor]] also wonders if they ever will be able to leave [[Petrichor V]].

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
Looking closely at the portrait art, the [[Scavenger]] can be seen with the following items:
[[Ukulele]], [[Medkit]], [[Disposable Missile Launcher]], [[Fire Shield]], [[Soldier's Syringe]], [[Mysterious Vial]], [[Gasoline]], [[Wicked Ring]], and some kind of green laser pointer, which does not seem to be a direct reference to any item.

During gameplay in *Risk of Rain 2*, the [[Scavenger]] may also make use of the [[Thqwib]] equipment.
