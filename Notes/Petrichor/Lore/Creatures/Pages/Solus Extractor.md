# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Solus Extractor]] is capable of stealing items from the players during gameplay. The log reflects this ability.

It seems that the [[Solus Extractor]] found and acquired an item which is described as `SHINY`, but after it loses this property it loses interest and starts searching for another item.
It is unclear what the purpose of this is, or if its looking for a specific item.

It's possible that the original purpose of the [[Solus Extractor]] was to extract resources from alien planets to assist the [[Solus Mother Unit]].
