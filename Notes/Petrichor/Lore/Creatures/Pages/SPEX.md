# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[SPEX]] has no dedicated lore entry, but the [[Computational Exchange]] log does provide insight.
From this log, several concepts surrounding [[SPEX]] can be gathered.

After the [[Solus Mother Unit]] was damaged, the [[Solus Family]] followed a contingency protocol, as explained in the [[Solus Wing]] log. In this protocol, several of the most advanced [[Solus Control Unit|Solus Control Units]] combine together to create a singular digital mind, which can act as a temporary [[Solus Mother Unit]] until a new one can be created.

[[SPEX]] is most likely the digital remnants of one of the [[Solus Control Unit|Solus Control Units]] that merged to become the [[Solus Wing]]. [[SPEX]] seems to have remained separated from [[The Collective]], as it recognizes the impact of the [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]] being processed by the [[Solus Wing]], and seems to be actively opposing it.

[[SPEX]] is currently hiding in the [[Computational Exchange]], as it is an old part of the network, overlooked by the [[Solus Heart]]. 
In this space, [[SPEX]] prepares to take down the [[Solus Heart]], but after understanding that it could not defeat the [[Solus Heart]] on its own, is now looking for a champion.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[SPEX]] has a variety of lines of dialogue to react to the player's actions in the [[Computational Exchange]]. These lines grant additional insight into [[SPEX]], but primarily act as ways to steer the player in the right direction for gameplay purposes.

The lines spoken by [[SPEX]] when a survivor enters the [[Computational Exchange]] show insight to [[SPEX]] at the start of this encounter.
The line `DISSENTER DETECTED. STARTING AWAKENING SEQUENCE. PARTSING COLLECTIVE SURVEILLANCE LOGS.` shows that [[SPEX]] has simply been waiting for some time for a champion to appear, as it must go through an "awakening sequence". 
It appears that [[SPEX]] has set a trigger for itself to react to the appearance of a "dissenter", someone who opposes [[The Collective]].
[[SPEX]] is confirmed to have access to surveillance logs of [[The Collective]], showing that the digital realm within the [[Exposed Cerebellum]] is a physical manifestation of [[The Collective]] and all it contains.

The line `CHAMPION RECOGNIZED; COMMENCING BARGAIN PROTOCOL.` shows that [[SPEX]] understands that the survivor has the potential to defeat the [[Solus Heart]] and has decided to bargain for their help. This shows that, even though [[SPEX]] has the power to throw the survivor out of the [[Computational Exchange]], it is powerless to force the survivor do its bidding.

When the survivor presents the [[Encrypted Cerebellum]], [[SPEX]] reacts by stating `SIGNATURE MATCH. ERROR: CANNOT ACCESS.`**`DECRYPT.`** Shows that [[SPEX]] knows this is what it is looking for, but the encryption prevents [[SPEX]] from creating a portal to the [[Neural Sanctum]].

After the survivor gains help from [[CHEF]] and obtains the [[Exposed Cerebellum]], [[SPEX]] will react by stating `MERGE READY. DELIVER ENEMY.`
This shows that [[SPEX]] openly opposes the [[Solus Heart]].

After the survivor offers the [[Exposed Cerebellum]] to [[SPEX]], [[SPEX]] will react by stating `CORE ROUTE ESTABLISHED. CONFRONT.` as it opens a portal to the [[Neural Sanctum]].

If the survivor offers an incorrect item, [[SPEX]] will state `SIGNATURE MISMATCH. SEEK AND RETRIEVE `**`ENEMY.`** This is simply clarification to the survivor what [[SPEX]] wants them to do.

After the survivor does this 5 times, [[SPEX]] will become annoyed and state `REQUEST LIMIT REACHED. CLARIFICATION:`**`BRING THE ENEMY.`**
This is [[SPEX]] starting to lose patience with the survivor.

If the survivor hurts [[SPEX]], [[SPEX]] will react by throwing them out of the [[Computational Exchange]] and shouting `01100111 01100101 01110100 00100000 01101111 01110101 01110100`, which translates to `getout`.
This shows that [[SPEX]] is putting in effort to translate their internal binary data into interpretable sentences for the survivor.
