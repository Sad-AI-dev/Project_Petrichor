# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a transcription of an audio fragment detailing the events surrounding the crew of `DropShip 9` who gathered at `randezvous point 9` in the [[Sky Meadow]].
After landing, the crew waited for `zeta squad` to meat them.
The use of dropships shows that this was part of either the [[UES [REDACTED]|UES [REDACTED] ]]or [[UES Safe Travels]] mission.

The crew of `DS-9` witnessed a [[Grandparent]] near the landing zone, where they kept waiting for `zeta squad`.
They appear to have all been killed by the [[Grandparent]] before `zeta squad` showed up.
