# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log represents the internal dialogue from the [[Alloy Worship Unit]] as it recounts it's transformation and the conversation that followed with members from the [[Alloy Family]].

The log shows how the [[Alloy Worship Unit]] was a [[Solus Control Unit]] once upon a time, before members of the [[Alloy Family]] repaired it.
This shows how members of the [[Alloy Family]] are intelligent enough to understand and repair complex electronics.

The conversation reveals various information about the origins of the [[Solus Family]].
First, the [[Alloy Worship Unit]] confirms that is originates from space.

Next, it is revealed that the [[Solus Family]] is reigned by an entity known as the [[Solus Mother Unit]], and that the reason for the [[Solus Family|Solus Family's]] presence on [[Petrichor V]] is that this [[Solus Mother Unit]] needs to be prepared. 
The [[Solus Control Unit]] log reveals that they came to [[Petrichor V]] in search of iron.

After that, the [[Alloy Worship Unit]] reveals that the [[Solus Mother Unit]] can (and possibly must) be piloted, and that the [[Alloy Worship Unit]] is capable of doing so. It is possible that any [[Solus Control Unit]] is capable of piloting the [[Solus Mother Unit]].

Finally, a declaration of love. It shows that the [[Alloy Worship Unit]] is capable of feeling something akin to emotion and shares a genuine connection with the [[Alloy Family]].
