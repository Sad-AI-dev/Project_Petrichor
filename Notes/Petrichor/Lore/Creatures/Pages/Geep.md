# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
Whenever a [[Gup]] is defeated or feels sufficiently threatened, it will split into 2 [[Geep]].
Once a [[Geep]] is defeated or threatened, it will split into 2 [[Gip]].

During this process, the color shifts from a red orange color to a more yellow orange color.
