# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a direct continuation of the [[Imp]] log, as an [[Imp Overlord]] receives news of the fate of the [[Imp]] party.

The [[Imp Overlord]] clarifies that the mission of the [[Imp]] party was to find and retrieve the `Artifact of Origin`.

The [[Imp Overlord]] is informed of [[Providence]] and decides to send reinforcements, in addition to sending themselves to make sure the mission does not fail. The tone in which the [[Imp Overlord]] exclaims that none shall resist them, not even god, shows that the [[Imp Family]] has a harsh hierarchical social structure.

What is notable, is that despite the [[Imp Overlord|Imp Overlord's]] assertions, they are not at the top of the hierarchy. That would be the [[Imp Emperor]].

The log mentions that the [[Imp Overlord]] needs to triangulate the position of the [[Imp|Imps]] in [[the White Plane]]. This is likely referencing the plane that hosts [[Petrichor V]], and thus the same one that hosts all of humanity.

`Hail to the Red Plane` seems to be a standard way of ending a message. This shows that the [[Imp Family]] likely rules over all of [[the Red Plane]].

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] encountered and killed an [[Imp Overlord]] at some point in their journey.
Of note is that the [[Imp Overlord]] splits as it dies before being dragged back through a portal.
This gives way for the theory that, when defeated, the [[Imp Overlord]] splits back into multiple smaller members of the [[Imp Family]].
The [[Imp Overlord|Imp Overlord's]] many eyes could serve as indication that the opposite is also true: multiple members of the [[Imp Family]] can merge together into a new [[Imp Overlord]].
