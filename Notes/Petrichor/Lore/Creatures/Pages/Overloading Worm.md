# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log is an entry of [[Galactic Fables]], specifically from volume 9.

The quite describes a hero, who rode on a mighty creature similar to the [[Overloading Worm]].
This may be in reference to [[Providence]], who rode on a [[Overloading Worm]] when saving other species across the universe, before bringing them to [[Petrichor V]].

It appears that, while on [[Petrichor V]], [[Providence]] typically rode on [[Gilded Wurms]], but when saving other worlds, he would choose to ride on an [[Overloading Worm]].

The title is a meta reference to the first game, where a [[Magma Worm]] could spawn as an overloading elite, which was a notoriously powerful combination.
