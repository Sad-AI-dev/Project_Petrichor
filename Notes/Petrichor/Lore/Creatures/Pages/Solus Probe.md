# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Solus Probe|Solus Probes]] are internally known to the [[Solus Family]] as `Mining Probes`. Their purpose was likely to mine and gather materials from distant planets in order to help sustain the [[Solus Mother Unit]].

The log describes what is likely a [[Solus Control Unit]] being requested to share its internal designs for the [[Solus Probe]].

The log reveals that the [[Solus Probe]] is capable of communicating directly with the [[Solus Mother Unit]] and offloading computational power to it. It can also act independently in case connection to the [[Solus Mother Unit]] is severed.
The [[Solus Probe]] is capable of flight and is equipped with a mining laser that can malt even heat-resistant compounds.
The [[Solus Probe]] is powered using solar power, which is stored in internal batteries. These batteries are typically charged while the [[Solus Probe]] is working.

Finally, the [[Solus Control Unit]] asks the entity that requested the information if they "love them too". This shows that the [[Solus Control Unit]] has started developing feelings and deviating from its original programming. This is a clear sign of it becoming part of [[The Collective]].

It is possible that the request is being made directly by the [[Solus Wing]], but this is left unclear.
