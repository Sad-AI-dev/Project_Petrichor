# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log contains a set of internal logs from a [[Solus Control Unit]] recounting the events leading up to the [[Solus Family]] arriving on [[Petrichor V]].
It is possibly also a direct prequel to the [[Alloy Worship Unit]] log.

The log reveals that the [[Solus Family]] arrived on [[Petrichor V]] due to the heavy iron presence detected on the planet.

After landing on the surface of [[Petrichor V]], the [[Solus Control Unit]] encounters the local population and starts killing it so it can start extracting the iron it came for. 
After a fight, the [[Solus Control Unit]] can't seem to find the iron that was previously detected. Immediately after, the [[Solus Control Unit]] loses its connection to the [[Solus Mother Unit]] and all its "Child Units", which are likely [[Solus Probe|Solus Probes]].
After losing connection with all other members of the [[Solus Family]], the [[Solus Control Unit]] is attacked and destroyed.

The end of the log, as well as the detail of the hull being destroyed, line up with the start of the [[Alloy Worship Unit]], suggesting that this [[Solus Control Unit]] may eventually be turned into the [[Alloy Worship Unit]] by the [[Alloy Family]].

Assuming this is what happened, it is possible that the heavy iron presence detected, the very reason for the [[Solus Family]] to come to [[Petrichor V]], was a ruse created by the members of the [[Alloy Family]] to trick the [[Solus Family]] into coming. The sudden loss of connection and subsequent damage dealt to the [[Solus Control Unit]] indicated a surprise attack from the [[Alloy Family]].

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
The title `Corrupted AI` likely refers to the fact that the members of the [[Solus Family]] are artificial and were corrupted by the [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]] processed by the [[Solus Wing]] and subsequently spread through [[The Collective]].
