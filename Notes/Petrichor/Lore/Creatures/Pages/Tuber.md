# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore"><span class="lore-text-dark">Field Notes:</span>

Like fighting a giant antlion, sometimes the best strategy was to lure them out by tossing spent magazines. I tried stones and other bait, but their responses to the bait seemed dependant on some unseen quality. # <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Tuber]], much like the [[Trokk]], hunts its prey by hiding in the ground and suddenly ambushing their targets once they come close enough.

[[The Survivor]] recalls some of their encounters of these creatures. They mention how they visually resemble the sort of small plants they took care off aboard the [[UES Contact Light]].

The best way to deal with a [[Tuber]] is to trick it into attacking a lure. They are rather clever and can tell apart certain vibrations. Due to this, according to [[The Survivor]], they can tell apart something like a small rock and a spent magazine based on some unseen quality.
