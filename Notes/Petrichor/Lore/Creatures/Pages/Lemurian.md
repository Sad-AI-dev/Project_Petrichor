This is the logbook entry of D. Furthen, naturalist for the UES [Redacted] in conjunction with the UES Research and Documentation of Outer Life. I am joined by my good friend Tharson, who is keeping me safe through this journey.

---------------------

While on an expedition in search of edible substances to take back to the ship, we happened upon a lone creature who displayed remarkable intelligence. Though it was initially surprised at our sudden appearance, it quickly turned hostile - and Tharson was unfortunately forced to terminate it.

I will describe its properties below. I have assigned its common name as “Lemurian.”

* Tall, bipedal creatures resembling lizards. They are adorned in rudimentary armor of an unknown metal, showing signs of high intelligence.

* Capable of breathing flame, even managing to hit Tharson in the chest from a distance with a fire ball. Tharson is OK from this encounter.

* Appear to be warm blooded, unlike most Earthen lizards. I have observed them emerging from underground nests all across the planet, even in cold areas where lizards would not dare to dwell. The flame sac that produces their fire breath must be able to regulate their body temperature.

* When observed from a distance, the Lemurians are social creatures, usually hunting in packs and conversing amongst each other.  Their language is currently undeciphered, but it appears to be complex enough to support song and rhythmic flow.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is an entry from the logbook created by [[D. Furthen & Tharson]], who were aboard the [[UES [REDACTED]|UES [REDACTED].]]
[[D. Furthen & Tharson]] were on a mission looking for food to take back to the wreckage of the [[UES [REDACTED]|UES [REDACTED] ]]when they encountered their first [[Lemurian]].

The assigned common name of the [[Lemurian]] is simple `Lemurian`. 

The [[Lemurian|Lemurians]] wear rudimentary armor and can spit fireballs.
They are likely warm blooded creatures, unlike most other lizards.

[[Lemurian|Lemurians]] are highly social creatures. They hunt in packs and can be seen conversing amongst the group. 
They have a unique language, the written version of which will be referenced to as [[Lemurian Script]] in this document. The language is complex enough to support song and rhythm.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore"><span class="lore-text-dark">Field Notes:</span>
The first sentient species I discovered on my unfortunate arrival. Around 7' tall, with scaly skin not different from that of a lizard. Bipedal. Aggressive on sight, minus a few exceptions. These Lemurians have an extremely powerful bite, but don't seem to employ weapons.

All species that I have encountered have armor on their shoulders and wrists, indicating a high intelligence. They seem to live in underground tunnels networked all over the planet, and can use these tunnels to ambush prey.

From what I've briefly seen, they seem to enjoy song and dance.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Lemurian]] was the first sentient species discovered by [[The Survivor]].
They stand roughly 7 feet tall, and visually resemble large lizards.

[[Lemurian|Lemurians]] carry armor on their shoulders and wrists indicating high intelligence.
The [[Lemurian Family]] lives in an expansive underground network of tunnels, which covers the entire planet.

They also have a complex culture, and can be spotted engaging in song and dance.
