# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] recounts an encounter with members of the [[Lynx Tribe]]. The members spied on him while he was briefly distracted, but they were never attacked by the creatures.

The [[Lynx Tribe]] is a group containing 3 distinct types of members: the `Scout`, `Hunter` and `Archer`. All use slightly different weapons and hunting strategies and were differing styles of wooden masks.
They call the jungles of [[Petrichor V]] their home and their intricately carved and decorated masks show a high level of intelligence. They level of technology is somewhat low, but members can be seen attacking using weapons such as spears and bows.
