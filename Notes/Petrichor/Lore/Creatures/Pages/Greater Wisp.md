*Greater Wisp* is a monster from both *Risk of Rain Returns* and *Risk of Rain 2*, belonging to the [[Wisp Family]].
For all monsters, see the [[Creatures.canvas|Creatures]] page.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a monologue spoken by a [[Grovetender]] as they create a [[Greater Wisp]]. This log shares many similarities to the [[Lesser Wisp]] log.

To begin, the [[Grovetender]] notes that `A storm is brewing beyound our garden`. The "storm" brewing outside [[The Garden]] is likely a metaphor for the incoming survivors of one of the UES excursions, but it is unclear which one. The most likely answer is that the [[Grovetender]] is referencing the recently struck down [[UES Contact Light]].

The mask used in the creation of a [[Greater Wisp]] is much larger, and baked twice. This gives the mask a metal-like quality.

A scoop of [[Powdered Lightning]] is given for power.
A coat of [[Dragonfoil]] is given for heat.
Two arms are given for dexterity.
Five scoops of a mysterious powder are given for spirit. This amounts to five times more spirit compared to the [[Lesser Wisp]].

Unlike the [[Lesser Wisp]] who are given no directive at the point of their creation other than to "enjoy life", the [[Greater Wisp]] is instructed to protect [[The Garden]]. This solidifies their position in the [[Wisp Family]] as protectors of [[The Garden]].

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>

Unlike the lesser Wisps, the Greater Wisp has two arms capable of typical motion. They burn a brilliant green which pierces straight though my sun visor.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] recounts their various encounters with the [[Greater Wisp|Greater Wisps]] on [[Petrichor V]].
They liken the creatures to be tougher than a [[Stone Golem]], faster than a [[Lemurian]] and harder hitting than both combined.

The addition of two arms are noted by [[The Survivor]] and their brilliant green light are noted to pierce straight through their `sun visor`.
The `sun visor` is likely something akin to sunglasses, but in the form of a visor.

The combat abilities of the [[Greater Wisp]] are noted on by [[The Survivor]], who mentions that they can accurately hit them at a distance of 200 meters.
