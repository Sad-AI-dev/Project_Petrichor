# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore"><span class="lore-text-dark">Field Notes:</span>
I found a group of red Lemurians resting in the dilapidated ruins with domesticated boars alongside them. I've never seen a red species outside of the flying types, though I doubt these are any more welcoming. With great spears and flamboyant helmets, they resemble cavalry of times past, and I'm not equipped to face any chariots head-on. In the distance I can see two of them sparring atop their beasts. They attack with sharp agility and acrobatic prowess that I hope to never experience.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] encountered a group of [[Lemurian Cavalry]] in the [[Temple of the Elders]].
The [[Lemurian Cavalry]] are red, much like the [[Evolved Lemurian|Evolved Lemurians]], but they don't have wings.

The [[Lemurian Cavalry]] have tamed a group of [[Boarlit|Boarlits]] to use as mounts. Their fighting style greatly resembles that of ancient cavalry forces. 
Notably, they make use of spears, unlike the [[Lemurian|Lemurians]], which prefer to use their fireballs and claws over carrying any weapons.

It is unclear if [[Lemurian|Lemurians]] can mature into [[Lemurian Cavalry]] or if they are a separate sub-species, much like the [[Evolved Lemurian|Evolved Lemurians]]. It is equally unclear if only [[Lemurian|Lemurians]] can mature into [[Elder Lemurian|Elder Lemurians]] or if the [[Lemurian Cavalry]] can mature in a similar way.
