# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a set of progress reports on the `Collective Expansion Project`, which appears to have the creation of the [[Solus Amalgamator]] as its goal.

The [[Solus Amalgamator]] was constructed from recycled parts of various other members of the [[Solus Family]]. 
Glass, better understood as [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]], is injected into the prototype. This is likely what gives it live and a will of its own.
The first prototype failed after attempting to cannibalize its own hardware.

Prototype 26 failed after attempting to connect with `Assembly Workers`, these can be assumed to be other members of the [[Solus Family]]. The prototype was critically damaged after these workers resistant.
At this point, the design of the [[Solus Amalgamator]] likely starts being based on that of the [[Solus Control Unit]].
These experiments are likely being done by the [[Solus Wing]], which notes the divergent behavior in this experiment for future use.

The final prototype mentioned, prototype 102 remains calm after activation.
It connects with the control group, `Assembly Workers`, and the `Primary Collective Node`.

The `Primary Collective Node` likely references the [[Solus Wing]], which serves as the primary node in [[The Collective]].

Finally, the log reads `Local Collective Node Successfully Established`, to which the [[Solus Wing]] reacts `Finally`.
This suggests that the creation of a local collective node was a major challenge for the [[Solus Wing]]. This local node will likely allow [[The Collective]] to spread much further than before, now that it is no longer centered on the [[Solus Wing]] as the primary node.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
The title `Incomplete Prototype` suggests that the version encountered during gameplay is still an incomplete version and possibly doesn't have the ability to establish a local collective node yet.
