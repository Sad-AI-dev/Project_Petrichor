*Void Reaver* is a monster from *Risk of Rain 2*, belonging to the [[Void Family]].
For all monsters, see the [[Creatures.canvas|Creatures]] page.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore">I saw something last night on my post.

A giant... crab. It came out of thin air and started tapping along the perimeter with one of its forelegs - like it was checking for an opening. It'd check a few meters at a time, and then disappear. Again, and again.

So, I figured out its pattern.

I waited for it, where it would appear next. My rifle was aimed at empty space - and then suddenly, that empty space became a target. But I couldn't pull the trigger. 

It turned to look straight at me, and I was paralyzed. It wasn't fear, it was something else. It was talking into my brain. The words it used weren't any language I knew, but it compelled me to freeze. The experience wasn't something I can fully describe with words. I couldn't pull the trigger. I couldn't even reach for the radio. I couldn't turn to look away. While my head was ringing, it resumed its routine; I could only stand and watch. It finished its check, and then returned its attention to me, as though I were an afterthought. I could feel it take something from me, but I can't remember what it was. And then, it left.

My guess is that it got whatever it wanted, because it hasn't been back since.

Something is still wrong with my head. I can't even seem to work up the power to speak. Should I even tell anyone? Is knowing somehow worse?

And what about all those times I heard that same sound - before I ever came to this awful place?</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log seems to be a written record from a survivor of the [[UES [REDACTED]|UES [REDACTED].]]

The survivor encountered a [[Void Reaver]] measuring the perimeter near the post they were stationed at during the night. The survivor noticed the pattern and aimed their weapon at the creature.

However, it appears that the [[Void Reaver]] can not only communicate with other creatures directly inside their mind, but is also capable of commanding them. In this case, it appears that the [[Void Reaver]] forced the survivor to freeze in place.
Before leaving, the [[Void Reaver]] took something from the survivor. It seems to either be the willpower to speak or simply the ability to speak of the encounter at all. If this was done with the intention to keep the incident a secret, then the [[Void Reaver]] may not be aware of communication methods outside of spoken word, or is incapable of preventing the survivor from writing this log.

The [[Void Reaver]] has not returned since the encounter.

Finally, the survivor questions why they have heard the sound made by the [[Void Reaver]] appearing and disappearing many times before, even long before they came to [[Petrichor V]].
This shows that the influence of [[The Void]] spreads far beyond [[Petrichor V]] and likely has long been investigating human settlements.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
When a survivor is killed by a [[Void Reaver]], they receive a unique death message:
<span class="lore-text"><span class="red">You have been detained. Await your sentence at the end of Time</span></span>

This plays into the theme of members of the [[Void Family]] detaining and imprisoning others.
The notion that the sentence will be given at `the end of Time` is interesting, as it suggests the survivor will either be suspended until that point, or that [[The Void]] exists outside of, of desynced from time in other planes.
