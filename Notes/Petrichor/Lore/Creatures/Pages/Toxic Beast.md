# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] encountered a [[Toxic Beast]] at a later point in their journey.
The [[Toxic Beast]] is primarily dangerous due to its enormous side.

The many scars on the [[Toxic Beast|Toxic Beast's]] were left there by the battles it survived. There is no mention of the toxic ooze spilling from the beast's orifices, or it's hollow eyes.
