# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log is an audio transcription of a voice recording made by a survivor who sighted a herd of [[Clay Dunestrider|Clay Dunestriders]] while they were lost somewhere in the deserts near the [[Abandoned Aqueduct]].

The behavior sighted likely is the [[Tar]] attempting to spread to and subsequently consume a new location.

The survivor mentions they still can't believe credit to how terrible their stay on [[Petrichor V]] has been. This gives credit to the idea that this survivor may have been on the [[UES Contact Light]].

The desert the survivor finds themselves in is described as endlessly vast and featureless.
