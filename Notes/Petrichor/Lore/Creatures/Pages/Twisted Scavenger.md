# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Twisted Scavenger|Twisted Scavengers]] are a unique collection of [[Scavenger|Scavengers]] that can be encountered in [[A Moment, Whole]].
There are 4 unique [[Twisted Scavenger|Twisted Scavengers]]:
- KipKip the Gentle
- WipWip the Wild
- TwipTwip the Devotee
- GuraGura the Lucky

As explained in the [[A Moment, Whole]] log, these [[Scavenger|Scavengers]] are loyal followers of [[Mithrix]], who are currently hiding in the [[A Moment, Whole]] realm to wait out the incoming war that [[Mithrix]] is about to start on [[Petrichor V]].

They served a major role in collecting information for [[Mithrix]] in preparation of this war.
