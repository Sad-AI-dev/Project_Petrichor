# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log contains a machine translation of a recording of cosmic noise that was recorded in the Stratosphere of some planet. Which planet is unknown, as it was censored.

The contents appear to be a set of instructions for how to reach [[The Void]].

The first step is enable the `engine`. After waiting for 34 `cycles`, a `field` will be produced. This is what will allow one to cross over to where [[The Void]] is located.

It appears that these are instructions for something akin to a spaceship, as an engine is mentioned, but because the word `engine` is a placeholder for a term that couldn't be correctly translated and the actual word `engine` is used later without it being a placeholder.
This suggests that this may be referring to a particular device, perhaps of non-human origin.

Regardless, simply turning the device on and letting it run for 34 `cycles` is all that is required to cross the planes.
It is unclear if human vessels are capable of this.

Instructions are given to configure the engine to destination `[NULL]` and to not worry about turbulence, flashing lights or any creatures that board the vessel.
The destination is likely a reference to the `[NULL] portal`, which can be found in the [[Bazaar Between Time]] and sends the player to the [[Void Fields]] when interacted with.
It also seems like members of the [[Void Family]] board vessels that attempt to cross to [[The Void]].

Once the vessel makes it to [[The Void]], another set of instructions is given:
Do not worry about
- The agents escorting you to your cell
- Fauna spotted in the distance
- The infinite sky
- The panic attacks you will have from attempting to perceive said infinite sky
- The [[Void Barnacle|Void Barnacles]] growing on the hull of your vessel

From this, various ideas can be gathered. First of all, those who enter [[The Void]] are immediately imprisoned by the members of the [[Void Family]], who state that they do this for your own safety.

The fauna in the distance may be in reference to the [[Void Whale]], which can be spotted in the [[Void Locus]] during gameplay.

The specific term "grow" is interested when referencing the [[Void Barnacle|Void Barnacles]]. It seems that much like real barnacles, the [[Void Barnacle|Void Barnacles]] simply begin growing on any suitable surface within [[The Void]], which includes space vessels.

Finally, it appears that it is common for beings not belonging to the [[Void Family]] it is common to get panic attack from looking at the infinite sky.
