# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log contains a set of internal logs from a [[Solus Prospector]].
The purpose of the [[Solus Prospector]] appears to be to acquire iron ore from beneath the surface. They are equipped with a drill to assist in this.

While mining, the [[Solus Prospector]] is interrupted as the surface starts to move. Assuming that the [[Solus Prospector]] was mining into the earth, and not some arbitrary creature, the most likely scenario is that the [[Solus Prospector]] was attempting to drill into a member of the [[Golem Family]].

The next line `1T H75 GL755` or simply `it has glass` likely refers to the fact that this is in fact a member of the [[Golem Family]], who were designed by [[Mithrix]] and produced by [[Providence]]. In this process, glass is used as a metaphor for [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]].

After attacking the member of the [[Golem Family]], the [[Solus Prospector]] kills them and starts the "acquisition" of the glass. This shows that next to their original goal of finding iron to repair the [[Solus Mother Unit]], due to the actions of the [[Solus Wing]], the members of [[The Collective]] are now in search of [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]].
