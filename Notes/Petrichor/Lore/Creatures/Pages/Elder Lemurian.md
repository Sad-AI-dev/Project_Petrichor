# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is an image that was translated by the [[FIELDTECH Image-To-Text Translator (v2.5.10b)]].
The original image was likely text written in [[Lemurian Script]].

The log reveals that the [[Lemurian Family]] was saved by [[Providence]] and were granted a space to live on [[Petrichor V]]. They created sprawling tunnels and inhabited great caves.

Early on, the [[Elder Lemurian|Elder Lemurians]] were in charge of construction of the living quarters, which were decorated with [[Tetrafoil]] and glass baubles. The young members of the [[Lemurian Family]] were in charge of charting paths through the many tunnels.

In honor of [[Providence|Providence's]] kindness towards the [[Lemurian Family]], they constructed the [[Temple of the Elders]] on the surface of the planet.
The [[Lemurian Family]] have rituals of song and dance to celebrate their salvation.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] encountered an [[Elder Lemurian]] in the [[Temple of the Elders]]. This [[Elder Lemurian]] was likely female and acted alone, as [[The Survivor]] speculates if she is a queen of the [[Lemurian Family]].

[[The Survivor]] goes on to speculate about the great network of tunnels under the surface of [[Petrichor V]]. This shows their curiosity about the planet and its inhabitants, mentioning that they would gladly give up some of their very limited supplies if that would let them explore the tunnels unnoticed.
