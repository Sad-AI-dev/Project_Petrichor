# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore"><span class="lore-text-dark">Field Notes:</span>
Sinking into an endless lake, I was caught off-guard by these huge cellular beasts. Bounding towards me, I can almost identify their internal anatomy behind thick membranes - How is it possible that some sort of microbe could get this large? Can this really be a single cell? Rough, thorn-like cilia coat its surface and it's flagellum serves as a razor sharp whip. The water doesn't seem to impede it at all, though its strange bouncing behavior may be a result of its attempts to pursue me under heavy water pressure. I'm weary of the force it seems to exert even within these conditions. My oxygen supply can't afford any leaks.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] encountered a [[Macrobe]] in the [[Sunken Tombs]], while deep underwater in the lake.
The [[Macrobe]] appear to be giant single-celled organisms, which makes its biology somewhat similar to that of the [[Gup]].

[[The Survivor]] worries about their oxygen supply being limited, a leak would prove fatal.
