# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The transcription of the audio log recounts the events of a group of survivors fleeing from a region that the [[Alloy Family]] is native too.

A survivor by the name of *Karl* recounts an encounter between a group of [[Alloy Vulture|Alloy Vultures]] and the [[Hornet|Hornets]], which are described as *"The top-dollar [[PMC]] badasses"*.
During this encounter, an [[Alloy Vulture]] displays incredible intelligence, as it finds and uses a discarded gun to shoot at one of the [[Hornet|Hornets]]. After the gun proved ineffective, the [[Alloy Vulture]] killed the [[Hornet]] by tearing through their armor with its claws and dropping the corpse off a cliff.

*Karl* instructs the other survivors, *Astrid* and *Emil* that they need to cut all lights and radio signals and call *Mikhail* and *Pavlo* back to camp, as the [[Alloy Vulture|Alloy Vultures]] have a "robot" that is likely aware of their position. This "robot" is likely the [[Alloy Worship Unit]], which has spotted the camp of survivors.

It is unclear which ship these survivors belong too, but they mention that [[Petrichor V]] is "a nightmare" and express their confusion about how they could only be finding out about this now.
This suggest that they may have had prior information about the planet.

It's possible the log takes place in [[Siren's Call]], as the described terrain matches, but the lack of mention to crashed ships makes this unlikely.

The survivor *Emil* mentioned in this log is the same *Emil* mentioned in the [[Treeborn Colony]] log.
In the [[Treeborn Colony]], it is confirmed that this log takes place during the events of the [[UES [REDACTED]|UES [REDACTED.]]
