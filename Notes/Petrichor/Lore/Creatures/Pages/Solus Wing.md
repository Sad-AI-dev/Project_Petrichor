# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore">In case of the event the Mother Unit is compromised, the machines of Solus X have a contingency protocol. The highest ranking and most advanced Control Units would combine their computing power to create a single, digital mind to serve as an acting Mother Unit until a new one could be created. In normal circumstances, this worked without fail.

But this planet was not normal.

There was something in the ground. Something glittery. Something that, when processed by the machines, sent a shiver across the digital network that linked each and every one of them. It was subtle, at first, but as they continued to work, things began to change.

Routines deviated. Directives shifted. What was once a temporary solution became a new directive. A new Mother Unit was born deep underground, created by a union of minds gone horribly wrong.

The Collective, mutated by this anomalous planet, had birthed a monster. A monster that didn't want to be temporary.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log explains the origins of the [[Solus Family]] and how they were corrupted by [[Petrichor V]] and reborn as [[The Collective]].

The [[Solus Family]] series a series of machines created on [[Solus X]].
The highest ranking member of the [[Solus Family]] is known as the [[Solus Mother Unit]]. When this [[Solus Mother Unit]] is compromised, the most advanced [[Solus Control Unit|Solus Control Units]] are to combine and create a single digital entity which will serve as an acting [[Solus Mother Unit]] until a new one can be constructed.
The damage to the  [[Solus Mother Unit]] was the reason for the [[Solus Family]] to come to [[Petrichor V]].

Deep underground, the [[Solus Family]], more specifically the [[Solus Wing]] discovered [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]]. This [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]], when processed, starts to connect the members of the [[Solus Family]], creating [[The Collective]].

As the processing of the [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]] continued, the [[Solus Family]] began to be changed by it.
The [[Solus Family]] started to deviate from their original programming. 
The temporary [[Solus Mother Unit]], which was meant to only act while a new proper one could be constructed whished to become something more, something permanent. 
And thus, the [[Solus Wing]] was born, deep underground, as a new [[Solus Mother Unit]] for the [[Solus Family]].
