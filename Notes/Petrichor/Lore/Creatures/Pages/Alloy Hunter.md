# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore">The committee stirred as Ses-Hath approached the altar. Shattered hulls and broken trees formed a crude rotunda around the three elders. Overhead, Solus probes drifted in feathered robes, their optics glowing faintly. Below, only the drums cut through the cries of the crowd.

She knelt before the elders. The drumming stopped. Silence followed. Vel-Thoth, the eldest, rose and began to circle her, talons scraping stone. Before her, a ceremonial probe descended, cloaked in silk and ash. From its chassis, a bowl emerged, filled with a fluid that shimmered like molten glass, refracting colors no eye was meant to see.

The elders lifted the bowl. She drank.

Magenta and lime streaked her beak. The crowd erupted.

_And the world widened._

She felt heat radiating from feathers in the crowd. Heard the failing servo of a probe overhead. A child’s breath caught beneath a cloak. Vel-Thoth’s limp carried the weight of generations. Even the moonlight had texture.

The glass burned through her, illuminating more than ceremony. It showed her the machine - buried deep beyond the ash hills - feeding on the same living glass now coursing through her. Forests crushed into powder. Rivers boiled down to steam. Its hunger shaped the horizon.

She rose.

Salvaged Solus parts crowned her in metal and wire. The Spear of Ra-Thun found her hand, heavy with intent.

The crowd stared.

Wings spread, eyes forward, she lifted from the rotunda into the night.

The engine would fall. The land would remember her name.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log recounts the events surrounding the transformation of the [[Alloy Vulture]] named [[Ses-Hath]] into the [[Alloy Hunter]].

The transformation ritual took place in a crude rotunda of shattered hulls and broken trees. 
This points to the ritual  taking place near the [[Pretender's Precipice]]. The shattered hulls are those of defeated [[Solus Control Unit|Solus Control Units]].

Present at the ritual are a large number of [[Alloy Vulture|Alloy Vultures]], referred to as a committee (fun fact: a group of vultures is called a 'committee').
At the center of the ritual site, 3 [[Alloy Vulture Elders]] are present. These are likely the leaders of the [[Alloy Family]]. The eldest of these [[Alloy Vulture Elders|Elders]] is [[Vel-Thoth]].

During this ritual, drums are played, showing that the members of the [[Alloy Family]] have an understanding of music.
Above, in the sky, fly several [[Solus Probe|Solus Probes]], decorated with feathered robes. These were likely created by the [[Alloy Worship Unit]].

A bowl filled with a fluid which shimmers like molten glass is presented and later consumed by [[Ses-Hath]].
The liquid is one of the [[Fundamental Elements]]: [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]], described as "refracting colors which no eye was meant to see". 
This is likely a reference to the [[Bulwark's Ambry]] log, where it is established that [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]] is near impossible to perceive, even to [[Providence]] and [[Mithrix]].

Consuming the [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]] awakens [[Ses-Hath]], beginning her transformation. 
At this moment "Magenta and Lime streaked her beak". This seems to be a visual confirmation of something, as the crowd erupts at this visual.

The log describes how [[Ses-Hath|Ses_Hath's]] senses become heightened, allowing her to become hyper aware of everything surrounding her.
A vision is shown to [[Ses-Hath]]. It shows her the [[Solus Wing]], deep underground in the [[Solutional Haunt]], beyond the "ash hills".
It is unclear what the "ash hills" are in reference too. Perhaps the [[Scorched Acres]] or a general reference to the area surrounding [[Pretender's Precipice]].

[[Ses-Hath]] is shown how the [[Solus Wing]] is feeding on [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]], and how it's actions are effecting the surrounding areas. Forests have been harvested and crushed to dust, rivers emptied and boiled to steam. 
The line "Its hunger shaped the horizon" is likely a reference to [[Conduit Canyon]]. As this is the greatest example of how the [[Solus Wing|Solus Wing's]] actions are starting to affect the environment.

The spear of [[Ra-Thun]] is the weapon that the [[Alloy Hunter]] uses. It's tip can be obtained in the form of the [[Faulty Conductor]].

Finally, [[Ses-Hath]] refers to the [[Solus Wing]] as "The Engine", and comments about how her actions will be remembered.

This long is continued in the [[Conduit Canyon]] log.
