# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a report of data found in an `Unknown Material Artifact`.
The contents are written from the perspective of a party of [[Imp|Imps]] that have come to [[Petrichor V]].

The [[Imp]] describes the planet as nightmarish, much like survivors later would.
A particular note about the long nights and unnaturally eclipses show that sunlight is scarce on the surface of [[Petrichor V]].
The fact that an [[Imp]] comments on the lack of sunlight, suggests that this is something they need or feel comfortable in, possibly suggesting that [[the Red Plane]] is lit by something similar to sunlight.

The [[Imp]] mentions that they find it appalling that they must scavenge and hunt for food. This shows that the [[Imp Family]] is a highly advanced society, one where hunting for food is considered uncivilized.

It is established that the air on [[Petrichor V]] is breathable, but smells foul. This suggests that either [[Imp|Imps]] require oxygen and therefore have something akin to lungs, or that they breathe something else. The note about smell shows that [[Imp|Imps]] have a sense of smell, and that [[Petrichor V]] smells different from [[the Red Plane]].

The [[Imp]] gives some insight into how they got to [[Petrichor V]] from [[the Red Plane]]. They achieved this using their ability to enter [[the Between Space]] and crossing through `the membrane`. 
Later, the imp mentions that what appears as them teleporting is, in fact, them briefly diving into [[the Between Space]].
It takes considerable effort to cross through `the membrane`.

The [[Imp]] mentions their encounter with [[Providence]], how his presence prevents them from returning to [[the Red Plane]] and how many of them were killed by him.
They also mention feeling most weakened when in his presence.

When not engaged in combat, [[Providence]] can be seem watching the moon. This is presumably the same moon where [[Commencement]] is located and [[Mithrix]] was banished too.
The [[Imp]] finds this behavior somewhat humorous.

At the end of the log, the [[Imp]] hopes that news of their situation will reach the [[Imp Emperor]], as they believe the [[Imp Emperor]] would be able to kill [[Providence]] with ease.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] expresses fear for the [[Imp|Imps]]. They find their sharp talons and singular piercing eye unnerving.

They note that, much like themselves, the [[Imp]] is hostile to all other fauna on [[Petrichor V]]. 
This shows that the members of the [[Imp Family]] are invaders on [[Petrichor V]], as they originate from [[the Red Plane]], and where not brought here by [[Providence]].

Additionally, the [[Imp]] is capable of something akin to teleportation. This frustrates [[The Survivor]], as humanity has yet to figure out the secret to teleportation.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">General Notes</span>
In *Risk of Rain*, the [[Imp]] is referred to as the `Black Imp`, this is likely to distinguish it from the [[Purple Imp]], and could be inferred as an unofficial extended name for the [[Imp|Imps]].

In the background of the portrait art for the [[Imp]] from *Risk of Rain Returns*, a distorted visage of [[the Red Plane]] can be seen. A variety of [[Imp]]-like creatures can be spotted. These are all members of the [[Imp Family]] that are never seen on [[Petrichor V]].
