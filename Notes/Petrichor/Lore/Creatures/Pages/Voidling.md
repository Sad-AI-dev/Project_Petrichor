# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore">We almost got rid of it.

It was too massive, with too much weight.  It took up too much room, and was too dangerous.  We tried to massage it into its surroundings - we felt we had captured its essence well enough.  But its words beat into our minds and we weren't allowed to realize that it had been keeping us here.

We had begun spending all our days staring at it until it became formless to us.  Whispers of meaning with no words travelled through our ears and strung us up like a necklace of fools.  Totally mesmerized we sat around it like a campfire as it cultivated each of us with its cold embrace.  One by one we were scanned, and we were made to present all we had with us:  Feathers from local species, masks from strange spirits, abandoned metal scraps and rusty old equipment.  It examined each individually, its iris flexing mechanically as it traced the silhouette of every artifact.

For a moment I snapped out of it.  I felt fear.  I wanted to scream out, to raise my weapon, but my body was wholly resistant.  Suddenly, the others all turned their heads to face me from across the circle, their eyes wide and faces expressionless.  I felt a sharp pain in my chest and my neck stiffened.  After its final scan it vibrated out of existance, its atoms disseminating into the air.  We were left frozen husks around a campfire long since turned to ash.

We almost got rid of it.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log recounts the tale of a group of survivors that attempted to defeat the [[Voidling]].

Unfortunately for them, the [[Voidling]] was simply too strong. It appears that the [[Voidling]] uses a similar ability to the one mentioned in the [[Void Reaver]] log, but on a larger scale. 
It uses sound, "whispers of meaning with no words", to influence and manipulate its target. It's capable of pacifying its target, allowing it to thoroughly scan the target before leaving them to die a long, gradual death as the target remains unable to move.

Even if one manages to briefly escape its influence, the body remains frozen. Additionally, the [[Voidling]] will simply reapply its influence, leaving the target no chance of escape before its done scanning.

The survivors presented various items they were carrying with them to the [[Voidling]]:
- `Feathers from local species`
  During gameplay, the only feather item the player can obtain is the [[Hopoo Feather]], but the `hopoo` bird is not native to [[Petrichor V]].
  A few birds can be spotted during gameplay, the most prominent of which would be the [[Alloy Vulture]]. It is possible that the survivors carried a feather from an [[Alloy Vulture]] with them.
- `Masks from strange spirits`
  This is likely in reference to the members of the [[Wisp Family]] and the [[Little Disciple]] item.
- `Abandoned metal scraps and rusty equipment`
  This is a bit too vague to pin down, but plays into the general state of the equipment provided to the survivors on [[Petrichor V]].
