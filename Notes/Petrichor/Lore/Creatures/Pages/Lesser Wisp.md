# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log is a monologue spoken by a [[Grovetender]]. The latter half contains half of a conversation with the recently created [[Lesser Wisp]], although the log does not contain their responses. This may be because the [[Lesser Wisp]] is incapable of speech, and uses some other method of communication.

The core ingredient for a [[Lesser Wisp]] appears to be a hardened clay mask. The [[Grovetender]] uses a kiln to temper the clay.

A coil of [[Denku rope]] is given for strength.
A strip of [[Edelwood]] bark is given for curiosity.
Finally an unidentified powder is given for spirit.

This mysterious powder appears the be the final ingredient in creating a member of the [[Wisp Family]].

The [[Grovetender]] notes that their purpose is to tend to [[The Garden]].

By the end of the conversation, the [[Grovetender]] lets the [[Lesser Wisp]] meet some friends. They note that they wish for the [[Lesser Wisp]] to "enjoy life". Notable, they state "this is my gift to you".
The Phrasing of parental figures giving a gift appears to be a theme in the wider risk of rain universe. In this case, that gift it explicitly the gift of life.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore"><span class="lore-text-dark">Field Notes:</span>
Characterized by their constant flame, the Wisps are not living beings. Each Wisp has a mask in its center, where all the fire erupts from. The Wisps are able to concentrate their heat into a long range heat blast, capable of scorching anything in its path. On taking heavy damage, the fuel powering the Wisp detonates, sending the mask soaring clear through the air.

How they function is a mystery to me. While I've seen 'deactivated' masks all around this world, the only time I've spotted Wisps is around me, as if someone creates them specifically to kill me..</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] had many encounters with the [[Lesser Wisp|Lesser Wisps]] on [[Petrichor V]], but never figured out how they are created.
[[The Survivor]] notes that the [[Lesser Wisp]] is not a living creature, rather an animated construct with a mask that the flames concentrate around.

The [[Lesser Wisp]] attacks by shooting a concentrated beam of fire at their target. On defeat, they detonate, sending the mask flying.

Notably, [[The Survivor]] has seen a ton of masks around [[Petrichor V]] that resemble those that the [[Lesser Wisp]] animates.
