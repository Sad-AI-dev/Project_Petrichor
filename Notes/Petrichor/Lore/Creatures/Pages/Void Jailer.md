# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore">Deep, deeper, yet deeper.

The only way to find such places is to go deeper.

Deeper into curiosity. Deeper into hubris. Deeper into the depths of creation.

And what you may find are the ones who are able to thrive in the depths, those murky dimensions and stilled spaces.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
This log appears to be describing [[The Void]] as a location and its greater place in the setting.

There is always a greater threat, a larger being, and to find these, one will have to search for them. During this search, it is inevitable to stumble upon [[The Void]], which is where one will encounter those that are able to not only survive, but *thrive* in the depths, the murky dimensions and stilled spaces.

The final paragraph gives perhaps the most important insight into [[The Void]]. Once one finds themselves in [[The Void]], they will quickly realize that all paths simply lead deeper into [[The Void]]. There is not escape, and [[The Planetarium]] is by no means the bottom.
