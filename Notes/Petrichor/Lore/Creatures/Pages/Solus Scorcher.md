# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log contains a set of internal logs produced by a [[Solus Scorcher]] as they fight various creatures on the surface of [[Petrichor V]].

It appears that the goal of the [[Solus Scorcher]] is simply to kill. Perhaps this will assist [[The Collective]] in their goal of obtaining more [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]].

The [[Solus Scorcher]] kills a [[Lemurian]], [[Beetle]], [[Alloy Vulture]], and a [[Hermit Crab]].
They also encounter a survivor along the way, but they fail to kill them. It is unclear if this survivor originates from the [[UES [REDACTED]|UES [REDACTED] ]]or [[UES Safe Travels]].
