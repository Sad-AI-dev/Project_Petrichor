*Lunar Chimera (Golem)* is a monster from *Risk of Rain 2*, belonging to the [[Lunar Family]].
For all monsters, see the [[Creatures.canvas|Creatures]] page.

# <span class="lore-title-inline">Lore</span>
<span class="lore">Your completions of my designs are uneasy, brother. Let me show you their purpose.

We begin with stone, silver, and fire. Take note of the ratios.

High speed. Speed is war. 

Twin cannons – with twin exhausts. We stream their exhausts, for volatility.

Quadrupeds are easier, and more stable. We maintain that.

And that, dear brother, is it. No dance. No song. These guardians do not need soul.</span>
# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log is a comment from [[Mithrix]] on the design of the [[Lunar Chimera (Golem)]], as they were created by him.

The log starts by [[Mithrix]] commenting on how [[Providence]] realized his designs for the [[Stone Golem|Stone Golems]] and how their purpose was lost in this process.

Unlike the [[Stone Golem]], the [[Lunar Chimera (Golem)]] was purely created for combat. They are faster, have more firepower and are more stable.

The main difference, which [[Providence]] added and [[Mithrix]] finds unnecessary, is [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]]. Machines of war have no need for dance or song after all.

The [[Lunar Chimera (Golem)]] greatly resembles the [[Stone Golem]] in design, but also takes various elements from the [[Temple Guard|Temple Guards]].
