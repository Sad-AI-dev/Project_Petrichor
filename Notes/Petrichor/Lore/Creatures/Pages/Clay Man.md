# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] encountered a [[Clay Man]] at some point in their journey, but makes no note of when or where this encounter happened.

It is pointed out that the [[Clay Man]] is exceedingly agile and one of the few creatures on [[Petrichor V]] that resembles a human. The fact that they are not, and merely resemble humans is a cruel reminder to [[The Survivor]] as to how lonely they truly feel on this planet.
