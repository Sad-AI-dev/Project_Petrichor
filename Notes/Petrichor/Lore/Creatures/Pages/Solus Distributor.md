# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Solus Distributor]] is a small construct buried into the ground capable of releasing [[Solus Mine|Solus Mines]] to dispose of enemies.
This log shows the internal console outputs from a particular [[Solus Distributor]], which seems to be slowly deviating from its original programming as it starts to become part of [[The Collective]].

The [[Solus Distributor]] starts by deploying [[Solus Mine|Solus Mines]] to respond to detected movement, then deploys them without cause and realizes that there is nothing to gain from this, thus the line `NOTH1NG M4TT3R5`.
