# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The log appear to be a set of internal logs produced by a [[Solus Invalidator]]. 
The machine seems to be experiencing excessive amounts of hatred towards all others as they attempt and repeatedly fail to fly.

The `Fleshbags` mentioned are possibly survivors from the [[UES [REDACTED]|UES [REDACTED] ]]or [[UES Safe Travels]].

Eventually, the [[Solus Invalidator]] attempts to locate the [[Solus Repair Unit]]. This is possibly part of its original programming, directing it to be repaired. This naturally leads to the theory that this particular [[Solus Invalidator]] is malfunctioning, of at least believes itself to be broken in some capacity.

The binary `01001000 01000001 01010100 01000101` translates to `HATE`
