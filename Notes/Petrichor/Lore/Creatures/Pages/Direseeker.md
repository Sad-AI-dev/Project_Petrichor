# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Direseeker]] is a unique [[Elder Lemurian]] that can be found in the [[Magma Barracks]]. 
Upon defeat, they have a chance of dropping the [[Lost Pickaxe]] item, suggesting that they were the one who killed the [[Miner]] during the events of the [[UES Contact Light]].

The [[Direseeker]] is the only [[Elder Lemurian]] that can be found in the [[Magma Barracks]]. Their unique red color scheme suggests that the [[Direseeker]] may be uniquely acclimated to the hot temperatures of the region. 
Alternatively, they may be a matured [[Lemurian Cavalry]], as they share their distinct red color.

The title can be interpreted in 2 different based on from which perspective the title is given.

If this title is an inherit comment on the [[Direseeker]], it could be gathered that they are a unique member of the [[Lemurian Family]] that fulfills a role within their society similar to that of a hitman. The [[Lemurian Family]] lives in a large connected network of underground tunnels, as such, the [[Miner]] may have been particularly dangerous to them. Sending the [[Direseeker]] may have been a logical response to this threat.

Alternatively, if this title is assigned by the survivors of the [[UES Contact Light]], this title would reflect a need for vengeance. This is the creature that killed the [[Miner]], and thus they must be tracked and killed.
