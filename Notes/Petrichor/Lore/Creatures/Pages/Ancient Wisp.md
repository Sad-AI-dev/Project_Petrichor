# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] questions if the [[Ancient Wisp]] is the creator of the other members of the [[Wisp Family]].
Unlike the other members of the [[Wisp Family]], the [[Ancient Wisp]] animates far more than just a mask. 
The [[Legendary Spark]] appears to be a pauldron that the [[Ancient Wisp]] was animating for example.

It is likely that the [[Ancient Wisp]] was created by the [[Grovetender|Grovetenders]], as they greatly resemble the other members of the [[Wisp Family]].
Their title `Banished and Chained` is noteworthy. Perhaps the [[Ancient Wisp]] was banished from [[The Garden]]? Chained may be in reference to a being chained to a physical form, as no visual chains appear on the [[Ancient Wisp]].

It is unclear what their original purpose may have been or if their relation with the [[Grovetender|Grovetenders]] is different from the other members of the [[Wisp Family]].
