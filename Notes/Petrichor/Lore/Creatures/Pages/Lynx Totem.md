# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
[[The Survivor]] spotted a [[Lynx Totem]] in the distance while in the [[Damp Caverns]].
They describe the [[Lynx Tribe]] to be made up of "little cat-like creatures", which can be seen performing some kind of cultural ritual around the [[Lynx Totem]].

The structure looks unlike anything else on [[Petrichor V]].

The boss title `N'kuhana's Faithful` shows that the members of the [[Lynx Family]] are followers of [[N'kuhana]]. The ritual that [[The Survivor]] sees them perform was likely religious in nature.
