# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The members of the [[Void Family]] hail from [[The Void]], a plane that currently serves as a prison.
Many members of the [[Void Family]] serve a purpose in capturing or detaining targets.

For example, the [[Void Reaver]] captures others through their implosion. the [[Void Jailer]] explicitly jails others. They can also be seem serving as wardens within [[The Void]].
Multiple complete planets can be spotted within [[The Void]], considering the title of the [[Void Devastator]] (`Destroyer of Worlds`), it is possible that the purpose of the [[Void Devastator]] is to capture entire worlds.

It is unclear what purpose the [[Void Barnacle]] plays, but they are likely a security measure. They are also mentioned to grow on any vessel that enters [[The Void]].

The [[Voidling]] is mentioned to scan those brought into [[The Void]]. It is possible that besides detaining, [[The Void]] also wishes to gather knowledge. It seems that this is the explicit purpose of [[The Simulacrum]].

It is unclear what the [[Void Whale|Void Whale's]] position within [[The Void]] is. They appear to be passive towards the survivors, so it is unlikely they hold a military role within the [[Void Family]].

Finally, there is the [[Void Infestor]]. These seem to hold an entirely different purpose from the other members, as they are only found outside [[The Void]] during gameplay. Instead, these are capable of taking over other creatures, forcibly aligning them with [[The Void]].
This appears to be an offensive technique employed be [[The Void]].
