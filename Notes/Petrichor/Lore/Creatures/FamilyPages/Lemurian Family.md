# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Lemurian Family]] is a highly advanced species, showing high intelligence, which have build a great civilization on [[Petrichor V]]. They live in a vast network of connected underground tunnels.
They have their own unique written language, dubbed [[Lemurian Script]] in this document, and have a rich culture containing song and dance.

The [[Lemurian Family]] has various sub species within it, each with a unique position within their civilization.

The [[Lemurian|Lemurians]] make up the largest portion of the civilization. Their young are called [[Humling|Humlings]] and they are capable of maturing into a menacing [[Elder Lemurian]].

The [[Lemurian Cavalry]] are physically similar to the [[Lemurian|Lemurians]], but their scales are a distinct red color. They seem to hold a militaristic position with the civilization. They are known to tame [[Boarlit|Boarlits]] and use them as mounts for combat.

The [[Evolved Lemurian|Evolved Lemurians]] likely split off from the civilization a long time ago. They are likely most closely related to the [[Lemurian Cavalry]], as they share their red color, but have since evolved to grow wings and sharp talons. They can be seen harassing other members of the [[Lemurian Family]].

The [[Direseeker]] is a unique [[Elder Lemurian]] that was last seen in the [[Magma Barracks]] where they killed the [[Miner]]. Their red scales suggests that they may be a matured [[Lemurian Cavalry]].
