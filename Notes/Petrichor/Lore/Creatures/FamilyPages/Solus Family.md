# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Solus Family]] is a group of machines guided by the [[Solus Mother Unit]] hailing from [[Solus X]]. After the [[Solus Mother Unit]] was damaged, the members of the [[Solus Family]] landed on [[Petrichor V]] intending to harvest the planet for iron in an effort to repair the [[Solus Mother Unit]].
The [[Solus Wing]] was created as a temporary [[Solus Mother Unit]] which was to be in charge of leading the [[Solus Family]] until a new [[Solus Mother Unit]] could be made.
However, after the [[Solus Wing]] started processing the [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]] laying deep underground, [[The Collective]] was born, which had no intention of replacing itself.

[[The Collective]] is internally opposed by [[SPEX]], who resides within the digital mind within the [[Solus Wing]], and the [[Alloy Family]], who have send the [[Alloy Hunter]] to [[Conduit Canyon]] in the hopes of defeating the [[Solus Wing]] and the [[Solus Heart]] who resides within.
