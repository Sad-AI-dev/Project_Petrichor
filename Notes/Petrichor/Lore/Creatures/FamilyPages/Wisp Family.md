# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Wisp Family]] is comprised of the [[Grovetender]] and the various wisps created by them.
All members of the [[Wisp Family]] appear to have some purpose related to [[The Garden]].

It is unclear how the [[Ancient Wisp]] relates to the other members of the family, as their are noted to be "banished".
