# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Boar Family]] is composed of the [[Toxic Beast]] and their offspring: the [[Boarlit|Boarlits]].
They are a rare find on the surface of [[Petrichor V]] and are primarily dangerous due to their large numbers and colossal size.
