# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Lunar Family]] is comprised of [[Mithrix]] and the constructs he created in his workshop in [[Commencement]]. [[Mithrix]] created these constructs with the intention of fighting a war with the inhabitants of [[Petrichor V]].

The family event shows how this war may begin. As the influence of [[Providence]] slowly fades after his death, [[Mithrix]] is free to start finding a way to leave the moon and destroy all his brother ever cared for.
