# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The members of the [[Macrobe Family]] appear to be based on single celled organisms. The [[Macrobic Predator]] seems to be a predator cell.
This shows that while related, the [[Macrobe]] likely does not mature into a [[Macrobic Predator]].

There is little to suggest how this species interacts internally, but they most likely act purely on instinct.
