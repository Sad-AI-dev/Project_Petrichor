# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Gup Family]] is entirely comprised of a single entity capable of splitting into smaller forms.

The [[Verdant Falls]] log suggest that the way these creatures come to be is due to a specific plant slowly taking over the host once sufficient amounts of it have been consumed, even after having been roasted.

It is unclear how this plant creates new seedlings, or if the [[Gup]] play a role in this process.
