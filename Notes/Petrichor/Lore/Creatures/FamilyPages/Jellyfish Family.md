# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The growth cycle is fairly simple in the [[Jellyfish Family]]. 
The small [[Jellyfish]] grows into a [[Young Vagrant]], which eventually matures into a [[Wandering Vagrant]].

It is mentioned that dozens, possibly well over a hundred, [[Wandering Vagrant|Wandering Vagrants]] can be seen floating through the sky above the clouds on a clear day on [[Petrichor V]].
