# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
something something imps die into existence and are looking for the `artifact of origin`, which will allow them to unborn themselves. They do this under the direction of the [[Imp Emperor]].
The [[Imp Family]] originates from [[the Red Plane]].

As established in the [[Imp Overlord]] log, the hierarchy of the [[Imp Family]] is very rigid, with those higher in the system often treating those under them rather poorly.

Various details give credence to the concept that the members of the [[Imp Family]] can merge together to become larger members of the family. Vice versa, they can also split into smaller members upon defeat, though they are typically pulled through a portal before this happens.

During gameplay, when the `artifact of origin` is active, it will spawn [[Purple Imp|Purple Imps]] and [[Imp Vanguard|Imp Vanguards]]. It is unclear if this is cannon with the greater lore.
