# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Scavenger|Scavengers]] are a strange species on [[Petrichor V]] that are not necessarily aligned with [[Providence]], although they also do not seem hostile to the other inhabitants of the planet.
They are best known for collecting the [[Items.canvas|Items]] that were brought here along with the survivors.

The [[Twisted Scavenger|Twisted Scavengers]] are a small group of loyal followers of [[Mithrix]].
