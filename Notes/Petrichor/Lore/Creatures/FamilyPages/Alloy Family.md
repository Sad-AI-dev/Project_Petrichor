# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Alloy Family]] consists entirely of [[Alloy Vulture|Alloy Vultures]], which a few notable individuals and close allies.

The [[Alloy Hunter]] (a.k.a. [[Ses-Hath]]) is a transformed [[Alloy Vulture]] that attained a greater form through a ritual surrounding the consumption of [[Fundamental Element: Soul|Soul]].

The [[Alloy Worship Unit]] is a [[Solus Control Unit]] that was repaired by the [[Alloy Vulture|Alloy Vultures]] and now acts in their interest.

Other notable individuals include Kur-skan the [[Heretic]], and the [[Alloy Vulture Elders]]. 
The elders are the leaders of the [[Alloy Family]], the eldest of the three is called [[Vel-Thoth]].

The [[Alloy Family]] works to protect the paradise that was created by [[Providence]] and will go to any lengths to achieve this. For this purpose, they also work together with other species, such as the [[Beetle Family]], as shown in the [[Treeborn Colony]] log.

The [[Alloy Vulture|Alloy Vultures]] are incredibly intelligent, as shown in the [[Alloy Vulture]] log, and are capable of understanding and repairing electronics, including being able to effectively use firearms.
