# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Lynx Tribe]] are a species of cat-like creatures found in the jungles of [[Petrichor V]].
The [[Lynx Totem]] is a religious structure that the [[Lynx Tribe]] ride around and perform rituals around.
The members of the [[Lynx Family]] are faithful followers of [[N'kuhana]].
