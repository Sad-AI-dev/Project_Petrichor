# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The [[Worm Family]] makes its home in the [[Helminth Hatchery]], where they nest.

The [[Scorch Worm]] is the youngest member of the [[Worm Family]], which eventually matures into the [[Magma Worm]].

The [[Overloading Worm]] seems to be a related creature, but it unclear how it mutates to command lightning. It is possible that it is a similar, but somewhat unrelated species.

Careful inspection shows that the [[Gilded Wurms]] are a separate species from the [[Worm Family]] even though they share many visual elements.
