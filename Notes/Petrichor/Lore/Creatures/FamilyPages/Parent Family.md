# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The growth cycle of the [[Parent Family]] is somewhat complex and explained in detail in the [[Planula]] log.
The [[Parent]] hatches from the [[Planula]] and cares for the other members.
The [[Parent]] matures into a [[Grandparent]], which are responsible for incubating the [[Planula]].
The [[Grandparent]] eventually matures into a [[Child]], which lay the [[Planula]].

The [[Child]] and [[Parent]] both have the ability to teleport distances, while the [[Grandparent]] can create a mass hot and bright enough to scorch any living matter in a large radius.
