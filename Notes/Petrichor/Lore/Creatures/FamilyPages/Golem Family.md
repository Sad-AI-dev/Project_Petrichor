# <span class="lore-title-inline">Notes</span>
The members of the [[Golem Family]] can be found all over [[Petrichor V]] and serve as one of the primary defenders created by [[Providence]] and [[Mithrix]].

The [[Stone Golem]] is created on the spot, while the [[Stone Titan]] rests deep underground and emerges when needed.

The [[Halcyonite]] are followers of [[Aurelionite]]. Together, they form a separate faction on [[Petrichor V]], which hopes to ally themselves with [[False Son]].
